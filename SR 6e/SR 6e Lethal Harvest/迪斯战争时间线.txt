[color=brown][size=16pt][b]迪斯暗影战争时间线[/b][/size][/color]
[size=14pt]
[b]2010年2月27日[/b]
据传这是“迪斯人”首次抵达物质位面的日子。他们起初仅觉得这个位面“勉强够用”，但在觉醒之后，对此有了更高的评价。

[b]2062年9月19日[/b]
据称此时迪斯人代表与阿兹特科技公司首次正式会面。以此为起点，迪斯人开始与多家企业与政府接触并结成同盟。阿勒什听闻一种新的泛位面威胁的传言，开始调查并招募盟友。

[b]2076年11月23日[/b]
“灰细胞”正式成立，旨在深入调查并对抗迪斯威胁。

[b]2081年9月26日[/b]
一场大规模的协同矩阵攻击突破了阿兹特科技公司紫外光主机的防御，获取了证实迪斯人存在的情报，以及“收割者网络”的真实本质与其“最终计划”。

[b]2081年10月10日[/b]
底特律战役期间，阿勒什开启了一道泛位面传送门，将来自泛位面分裂器的毁灭性能量引导至迪斯位面。能量未能摧毁该位面，反而加速其法力流失。这迫使迪斯人加快推进“最终计划”。灰细胞、迪斯人及其盟友之间的暗影战争由此加剧。

[b]2082年11月2日[/b]
“迪斯暗影战争”正式爆发，以“死亡周”为起点。在接下来的四个月中，迪斯人与抵抗者势力在全球各地展开多次战斗与冲突，迪斯人获得显著战略优势。

[b]2082年11月23日[/b]
“抵抗者运动”诞生，并启动“复仇女神计划”。亲迪斯阵营在抵抗者行动打击下转为防御态势，专注于保护“收割者网络”。尽管如此，部分亲迪斯组织与单位仍保持进攻状态，继续猎杀抵抗者及其盟友。

[b]2083年3月19日[/b]
“德尔塔-41惨剧”发生，迫使抵抗者改变对迪斯人及其盟友的应对策略。多项应急计划制定，抵抗者开始动用一些颇具争议的新武器。

[b]2083年3月21日[/b]
亲迪斯势力继续追踪并猎杀抵抗者，阿兹特科技公司仍是主力，但亦调动了多个其他单位参与。

[b]2083年3月24日[/b]
一名代号“特工辛”的五行公司行动人员叛逃至抵抗者阵营，并提供了所有已知收割者设施位置的情报(即“神圣地图”)。抵抗者高层制定四项作战计划并开始准备，同时继续搜寻破解迪斯密码的方法。

[b]2083年4月12日[/b]
抵抗者第304特遣队在阿兹特兰－尤卡坦边境附近首次发现“移动收割者”。同时首次正式记录到“迪斯人战争形态”个体。联络公司的激进策略被揭露，引发抵抗者运动内部摩擦。

[b]2083年4月27日[/b]
全球法力风暴频发，澳大利亚尤为严重。媒体将其描述为自然现象，实为迪斯“收割者”与“移动收割者”测试所致。抵抗者被迫转向打击次要目标，无法直接攻击收割者网络。

[b]2083年4月30日[/b]
毛茛召开她臭名昭著的记者会，引发主流媒体猛烈反弹。地平线公司借混乱之机搜集更多情报与攻击目标。从此起，战斗愈加激烈，搜捕“移动收割者”与破解迪斯密码的行动全面升级。阿兹特克“特殊项目”部门主管阿曼多·萨拉查开始策划公司内部政变。

[b]2083年5月1日[/b]
一次法力风暴席卷几乎整个澳大利亚大陆。成千上万人成功撤离，其余地区全面封锁并失联。

[b]2083年5月13日[/b]
西雅图的暗影狂奔者找到破解迪斯人语言与密码的最终关键。超统神经网络公司的抵抗者密码分析师开始着手研发实用解码器。迪斯人加大力度清除关键抵抗者目标与小组，首次大量使用“奇美拉”参与正面进攻。抵抗者开始进行反攻及实施预案。

[b]2083年6月11日[/b]
巨龙希尔格现身麦德林上空，这是他自亚马逊战争结束以来首次出现。数日后，希尔格开始攻击部分迪斯目标。多位巨龙之声在德意志联邦的一座S-K设施中会面。

[b]2083年6月14日[/b]
“天钩”太空电梯发生惨剧。初步报告称为事故，后被重新定性为恐袭。阿兹特科技总裁弗拉维娅·德拉罗萨与两位董事会成员身亡。阿兹特科技大幅加大对抵抗者目标的攻势，导致了暗影战争中最为惨烈的战斗之一。

[b]2083年7月9日[/b]
所有迪斯部队被召回，转入防御态势，围绕收割者网络构筑防线。借助新解码器，抵抗者终于破解迪斯人密码，并研发出安全关闭与摧毁收割者网络的方法。各地方案根据区域力量、能力及威胁等级不同而有所差异。与此同时，三津滨公司在收割者及其防御上投入了大量资金与物资。

[b]2083年7月12日[/b]
全球法力风暴再度加剧。来自五行的反迪斯消息源称，“最终计划”将在十日内启动。同一天，多位巨龙亲赴阿勒什的巢穴，与其商议参与对抗迪斯人之战的具体分工。

[b]2083年7月21日[/b]
时间紧迫，抵抗者下达最终命令，全面启动代号“炽天使”、“数据轰炸”、“堡垒”、“驱逐令”与“冷酷怒火”的多项作战计划，全面应对迪斯人威胁。第六纪命运之战拉开帷幕。

[b]2083年7月23日[/b]
所有抵抗者行动完成，抵抗者部队被命令停火但保持警戒，未发生任何报复。所有浮士德、非源自物质界的奇美拉、迪斯人及被困在物质界的其余个体全部开始死亡。全球法力风暴于约二十小时后全部消散。此日被追认为“迪斯影子战争”正式结束日。

[b]2083年7月23日至25日[/b]
全球法力场剧烈波动，引发公众恐慌，担忧澳大利亚式魔潮再度爆发。

[b]2083年7月26日[/b]
受法力场剧变影响，物质界所有魔法全面失效。大多数精魂返回星界，所有双重本质或觉醒个体均受到影响。媒体迅速将此事件称为“去觉醒化”。

[b]2083年8月5日至10日[/b]
魔法开始逐步回归物质界。受影响者逐渐恢复正常，但觉醒者普遍反映魔法“感觉起来不一样了”。各地开始全力重建因战争与(非)自然灾难所造成的破坏。
[/size]

[color=brown][size=16pt][b]THE DISIAN SHADOW WAR TIMELINE[/b][/size][/color]
[size=14pt]
[b]February 27, 2010[/b]

Rumored to be the Disians’ arrival on the material plane. Originally finding it merely adequate for their needs, they upgraded their opinion after the Awakening.

[b]September 19, 2062[/b]

Allegedly the first official meeting between Disian representatives and Aztechnology. Using this as a springboard, the Disians meet and form alliances with several other corporations and governments. Arleesh hears rumors of a new extraplanar threat and begins to investigate as well as recruit allies.

[b]November 23, 2076[/b]

Grey Cell is officially formed to further investigate and counter the Disian threat.

[b]September 26, 2081[/b]

A large-scale, coordinated cyberattack penetrates Aztechnology’s ultraviolet-rated hosts, obtaining data that confirms the existence of the Disians, the true nature of the Harvester Network, and their “Final Project.”

[b]October 10, 2081[/b]

During the Battle of Detroit, Arleesh opens a metaplanar portal and channels destructive energy from metaplanar disruptors into the Disian metaplane. Instead of destroying it, the energy hastens its rate of mana loss. This forces the Disians to accelerate plans for their Final Project. Subsequent fighting in the shadows increases between Grey Cell, the Disians, and allies on both sides.

[b]November 02, 2082[/b]

The Disian Shadow War officially starts with the Week of Death. The next four months are a series of engagements and skirmishes between Disian and Defender forces worldwide, with the Disians gaining a significant strategic advantage.

[b]November 23, 2082[/b]

Birth of the Defender Movement and implementation of Project Nemesis. Pro-Disian forces transition to a defensive posture in the wake of Defender operations, focusing on protecting the Harvester Network. Although some pro-Disian groups and units remain on the offensive and continue to actively hunt Defender forces and allies.

[b]March 19, , 2083[/b]

The Tragedy of Delta-41 occurs, forcing Defenders to change their strategies against the Disians and their allies. Several contingency plans are created as new, sometimes questionable, weapons continue to be deployed by the Defenders.

[b]March 21, 2083[/b]

Pro-Disian forces, spearheaded by Aztechnology but employing several other forces, continue to hunt Defender forces in the shadows.

[b]March 24, 2083[/b]

A Wuxing operative, known only as “Agent Xin,” defects to the Defenders and provides information regarding all known Harvester Locations (a.k.a. The Divine Map). Defender leadership decides on four operational plans and begins preparations while continuing to search for Disian code cypher.

[b]April 12, 2083[/b]

The first Mobile Harvesters are discovered near the Aztlan-Yucatan border by Defender Task Force 304. First official sighting of a Disian war form. Reports of Renraku’s methodologies begin to surface, causing friction within the Defender Movement.

[b]April 27, 2083[/b]

Global manastorms increase dramatically, most notably in Australia. Played off as natural phenomena in the media, the increase in storm activity is a result of Disian Harvester and Mobile Harvester testing. Defenders are forced to concentrate on secondary targets instead of assaulting the Harvester Network directly.

[b]April 30, 2083[/b]

Buttercup hosts her infamous press conference, and the mainstream media reacts negatively. Horizon uses the chaos to obtain more intelligence and identify targets. Beyond this date, fighting intensifies as the hunt for Mobile Harvesters and the key to the Disian codes ramps up. Aztechnology’s Special Projects lead, Armando Salazar, begins plans for an internal corporate coup.

[b]May 01, 2083[/b]

A manastorm surge covers almost the entire continent of Australia. Thousands manage to evacuate; the rest of the continent is locked down and all communication lost.

[b]May 13, 2083[/b]

Shadowrunners in Seattle find the final key to deciphering the Disian language and codes. Defender codebreakers from Transys Neuronet begin working on a usable cypher. Disian forces double down on their efforts to eliminate key Defender targets and cells. This includes using chimeras in larger numbers, often in direct assaults. In response, the Defenders initiate counter-offensives and other contingency plans.

[b]June 11, 2083[/b]

The great dragon Sirrurg is spotted in skies over Medellín, his first sighting since the end of the Amazonian War. Days later, Sirrurg begins attacking select Disian targets. The voices of several great dragons meet at an S-K facility in the AGS.

[b]June 14, 2083[/b]

Tragedy at Skyhook Space Elevator. Initially reported as an accident, reports later classify it as a terrorist attack. Aztechnology president Flavia de la Rosa and two other board members are killed. Aztechnology increases its offensive operations against Defender targets in retaliation, which leads to some of the most brutal fighting of the shadow war.

[b]July 09, 2083[/b]

All Disian forces are recalled and move into defensive positions around the Harvester Network. Using their new cypher, the Defenders are able to finally crack the Disian Codes and develop methods to safely eliminate and shut down the Harvester Network. These methods will vary depending on regional forces, their capabilities, and the threat they pose, but MCT has also put a tremendous amount of money and material into the Harvesters and their defenses.

[b]July 12, 2083[/b]

Manastorms across the globe increase in frequence and intensity. Information from an anti-Disian source in Wuxing indicates that the Final Project is set to begin within ten days. The same day, several great dragons meet with Arleesh in person at one of her layers to discuss their part in the upcoming battle with the Disians.

[b]July 21, 2083[/b]

With time running out, the Defenders give the final go-order for Operations: Saraphim, Data Bomber, Rampart, Eviction Notice, and Cold Fury as part of their plan to deal with the Disian threat. The battle for the fate of the Sixth World begins.

[b]July 23, 2083[/b]

All Defender operations have concluded, all Defender forces are ordered to stand down but stand by for possible retaliation; none occur. All known Faustians, chimeras not originally from the material plane, and any Disians trapped on the material plane start to die. All manastorms worldwide fade approximately twenty hours later. Date is retroactively declared the end of the Disian Shadow War.

[b]July 23-25, 2083[/b]

Across the world, the manasphere experiences massive fluctuations. Public begins to panic, fearing a second wave of Australia-like surges.

[b]July 26, 2083[/b]

As a result of the massive upheavals in the manasphere, all magic stops working on the material plane. Most spirits return to the astral, all dual-natured and Awakened beings are affected in some manner. Media quickly dubs this event “The Un-Awakening.”

[b]August 05-10, 2083[/b]

Magic begins returning to the material plane. Those affected start to return to a more normal state of being. Awakened individuals notice that magic “feels different” than before, however. Rebuilding and repair from fighting and (un)natural disasters begins in earnest.
